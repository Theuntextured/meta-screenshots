# Meta Screenshots

> [!WARNING]
> WORK IN PROGRESS!

Stop losing track of where your best Minecraft moments happened. **Meta Screenshots** is a lightweight mod for Forge 1.20.1 that upgrades your screenshot system with smart location tracking and seamless map integration.

[Image Placeholder: Demo]

## What it Does for You

* **Location Memory**: Every screenshot you take automatically "remembers" exactly where you were standing (X, Y, Z), which direction you were looking, and what time of day it was.
* **Xaero's World Map Integration**: Your screenshots can appear directly on Xaero's World Map, allowing you to click an icon on the map to see exactly what that location looked like when you visited.
* **Instant Sharing**: Tired of digging through folders? The mod can automatically copy your screenshot to your clipboard the moment you press F2 so you can paste it straight into Discord.
* **Smart Organization**:
    * **World-Aware**: It knows the difference between your various singleplayer worlds and multiplayer servers, even if you rename your save folders.
    * **Server Support**: Works on multi-world servers by keeping track of the Server IP.
* **Performance First**: It creates tiny thumbnails in a subfolder so that when you eventually browse your collection in-game, everything loads instantly without turning your computer into a glorified frying pan.

## Installation

1.  Make sure you are running **Forge 1.20.1**.
2.  Download the latest release from [GitHub](https://github.com/Theuntextured/meta-screenshots/releases).
3.  Drop the `.jar` file into your `mods` folder.
4.  (Highly Recommended) Install **Xaero's World Map** to see your screenshots on your map!

## Settings

You can customize the mod via your client config file:
* **Enable/Disable**: Toggle the entire mod on or off.
* **Clipboard**: Choose whether or not to automatically copy every photo you take.
